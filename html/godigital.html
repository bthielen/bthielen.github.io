G/O Digital Logo

about the piece

I created this logo for a real company, G/O Digital, as part of a pitch competition. Our pitch was for the company to replace their current logo. I added the upward arrow as an element of the 'G' to represent the company's digital marketing mission - to increase impressions, turnover and revenue of other brands. Our pitch placed 3rd out of 10 pitches.

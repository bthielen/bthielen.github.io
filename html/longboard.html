Longboard

about the piece

I disassembled an old longboard from Craigslist and sanded the wooden deck. I sketched about 30 different abstract designs on paper, then freehanded the final design's outline in pencil on the deck. I filled in the lines with colorful acrylic paint.
For the underside of the deck, I used masking tape to make blocks of color. I designed the mountains and my initials (BT) in Adobe Illustrator and laser cut them into stencil paper. Then I spray painted over the stencil. Afterward, I sealed the entire project with an acrylic sealant and added clear griptape to the top of the deck.

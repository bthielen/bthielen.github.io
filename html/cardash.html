CarDash Case Study

Objective

Redesign CarDash's partner page to boost user trust in their partner system and create more site conversions directly through the partner pages.

About CarDash

CarDash is a concierge service that seeks to redefine the automotive repair industry. It acts as a liason between busy customer and repair centers, picking up your car and dropping it off when the repairs are done.

The Original Page

  • No navigation relating to the main site - doesn't build trust
  • Hamburger menu on desktop isn't immediately understood to be navigations
  • Large chunk of paragraph text - not easily scannable
  • Inconsistently sized images
  • Only 1 instance of a call to action
  • Map at the bottom detracts from the fact that it's a concierge service

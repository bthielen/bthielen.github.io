Dancin' Devils

about the piece

I created this poster for my friend's on-campus club event. The event featured the Just Dance Wii game, so I created a poster with bright colors and movement to mimic the games' own branding. I added neon colors on a black background to give the impression of a disco, nightclub atmosphere.

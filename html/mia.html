#hirebethany

about the piece

After relocating to the Bay Area, I decided to re-think my approach to finding a job. Instead of applying to job reqs, I created a video campaign on LinkedIn using paid ads and social media to find a job.

I illustrated Mia and the video's background by hand. I used Adobe Character Animator to voice and animate her, turning my illustration into a video.

Pups At Poly

about the piece

Created for the second annual "Pups at Poly" ASU campus event, with trained therapy dogs for stressed out students. The first event was such a hit with the students that I thought of the second event like a highly-anticipated movie sequal, designing its marketing like a movie poster.
